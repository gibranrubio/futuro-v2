How it works

One simple account. One link. A lifetime of compounding.

Futuro wraps all the complex investing rails into a calm experience for parents. You focus on your child. We handle the rest.

Step 1

Open your child’s account

Create a custodial investing account in minutes. Add your child’s name, birthdate and a photo that makes you smile.

Step 2

Share a private gift link

Futuro generates a unique link for your child. Parents, grandparents and friends can send gifts straight into the account.

Step 3

Gifts invest automatically

Every contribution is invested into a simple, diversified portfolio through our regulated partner. No stock picking. No stress.

The idea

Small gifts today. Big options later.

A thousand dollars at birth can become many times that by adulthood. Add birthdays, holidays and small monthly contributions and the curve gets steeper.

Futuro turns those moments into one simple habit. Every time someone says “I love this kid” you have a way to turn that love into a real asset.

Why this matters

The slow plan that lets your kids breathe.

Most of us did not grow up with a safety net. We learned to hustle, to figure things out, to carry more weight than we should have. There are a thousand ways to make money and take risk. Futuro is not one more gamble. It is the slow, boring, smart plan that rides the broad American market for 18 to 30 years. It will not change your life this year. It can quietly change your child’s life later.

Even the best skydivers in the world, the ones who jump every day, always carry two parachutes. Not because they expect the first one to fail, but because real risk demands a backup.

Your life is the main parachute. You build businesses, you switch careers, you buy or rent, you invest in different ways. Futuro is the second parachute for your child. A simple S&P 500 habit that does not depend on you picking the right stock or guessing the right moment. It just needs time.

At first it feels small and almost invisible. A little money set aside, a link shared at birthdays and holidays. Years pass. The balance grows. One day your child is 18 or 25 and there is something real there. A down payment. A runway. A base.

You are not just giving them money. You are giving them breathing room. You are giving them permission to make choices from a place of stability instead of panic. You are taking away the quiet expectation that one day they will have to rescue you.

You can still swing big with the rest of your life. This is the one place where you choose slow, steady and safe on purpose.

Plan B that compounds quietly

Let your main life be risky, creative and bold. Futuro is the quiet backup that just keeps buying the market for your child, year after year.

Take the weight off your kids

Many of us expect to help our parents financially. This is one way to flip that story. You build a base so your child can focus on their own path.

A habit your future self will love

You will not remember every toy you bought. You will remember that you set up a system that kept working while you were busy living.
