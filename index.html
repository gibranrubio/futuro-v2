Gifting

No toys please, send compounding instead.

We appreciate you thinking of our little one. Instead of toys or more baby items, we are asking friends and family to contribute to a long term investing account that buys a simple S&P 500 fund for our child.

A different kind of gift

Most toys are loved for a season then end up in a closet. We are trying something quieter and longer term. Every gift goes into one S&P 500 fund and stays invested for years. Nothing fancy, no trading, just ownership that compounds in the background while our child grows up.

A small gift today can be worth much more by the time they are 18 or 30. If you would like to be part of that, you can use the contribution link we share in our invite.
